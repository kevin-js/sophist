How to Tutor.
Accept a tutee and get tutoring.
1   Sign up and create a profile, entering your rates, experience, and availability.
2   Tutees will request sessions. You can accept or deny depending on their rates and availability.
3   During the session, hit "Start" on the Session Timer, and "End" to automatically calculate your payment.
